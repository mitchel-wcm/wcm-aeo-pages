Trade-In & Selling
Trading in your vehicle is simple. Start by using our online trade valuation tool at wilsoncountymotors.com to get an estimated value, then bring your vehicle in for a final in-person appraisal. Our team reviews the condition, mileage, and current market value and presents you with a firm offer.

If you're purchasing a new or pre-owned vehicle, the trade-in value is applied directly to your deal. The whole process typically takes less than 30 minutes.
Yes. You do not need to buy a vehicle from us to sell us yours. We will purchase your car, truck, or SUV outright — no strings attached. Just bring it in for an appraisal and we'll make you an offer on the spot. Whether you're downsizing, upgrading somewhere else, or just looking to turn your vehicle into cash, we're happy to make an offer.
